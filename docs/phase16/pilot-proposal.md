# Pilot Proposal

## Objective

Run a controlled revenue intelligence pilot that shows how eTIMS, iTax-style returns, customs, withholding, PAYE, settlement, and county own-source revenue records can produce explainable risk priorities, evidence packs, and recovery dashboards.

## Recommended Pilot Tracks

1. KRA compliance intelligence: VAT output mismatch, import-to-sales mismatch, WHT mismatch, PAYE anomaly, and sector margin deviation.
2. County/public finance assurance: payment collection versus settlement reconciliation, missing settlement detection, delayed settlement detection, and wrong-account detection.

## Scope

- Use synthetic data for demos and approved pilot data only after written authorization.
- Load records through existing ingestion and validation flows.
- Produce risk signals, tax gap estimates, cases, evidence packs, nudges, executive analytics, and ROI outputs.
- Review all model-assisted outputs with an officer before any enforcement step.

## Success Measures

- Dashboard outputs reconcile to source records.
- Evidence packs can be generated live for sample cases.
- Pilot ROI workbook ties to dashboard recoverable-tax and recovery figures.
- Demo users and permissions work for executive, officer, admin, analyst, and auditor roles.
- No real taxpayer data is represented as demo data.

## Delivery Plan

- Week 1: confirm pilot owner, scope, data categories, and demo users.
- Week 2: load synthetic or approved pilot data and validate quality.
- Week 3: run risk, reconciliation, case, evidence, and dashboard workflows.
- Week 4: review ROI, security, privacy, procurement path, and next-step decision.
