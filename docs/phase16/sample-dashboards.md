# Sample Dashboards

## Executive Overview

Answers: What is the estimated exposure, how much is recoverable, and where should leadership focus?

## Executive Analytics

Answers: Which sectors, regions, and tax heads show the biggest gaps, and how does recovery compare to assessment?

## Risk Queue

Answers: Which taxpayers or revenue channels should officers review first, and why?

## Settlement Monitor

Answers: Which collections were not settled, were delayed, were duplicated, or went to the wrong account?

## Pilot Readiness

Answers: Is the product ready for a buyer conversation, what is the expected ROI, and what documents can be exported?

## Governance

Answers: Are roles, permissions, audit logs, retention, privacy, and export controls ready for pilot review?
