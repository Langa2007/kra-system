# Sample Evidence Packs

Evidence packs are generated live from cases in the demo environment. They should show only synthetic data unless an approved pilot dataset is being used.

## Required Contents

- Case number and taxpayer or revenue channel label.
- Rule or reconciliation exception that triggered the case.
- Period under review.
- Observed amount, declared or expected amount, estimated gap, and confidence.
- Source records used by the rule.
- Officer recommendation.
- Generation timestamp and user.

## Demo Examples

- VAT output mismatch for a synthetic taxpayer.
- Import-to-sales mismatch for a synthetic logistics taxpayer.
- Missing settlement for a synthetic county payment channel.

## Guardrail

Do not present generated evidence as an enforcement finding. It is an officer-review pack for prioritization, explanation, and controlled follow-up.
