# Pricing And Procurement

## Pricing Model

Recommended model:

- Annual software license.
- Implementation and configuration fee.
- Training and support fee.
- Optional success-based component only where procurement rules permit.

## Buyer Routes

- Controlled proof of concept under innovation, ICT, or reform-program approval.
- County own-source revenue pilot with implementation services.
- Integrator-led deployment where this platform is the intelligence layer.
- Annual government software license after pilot acceptance.

## Procurement Notes

- Keep pilot scope small enough to approve, audit, and evaluate.
- Avoid promising enforcement outcomes from model output alone.
- Present ROI as estimated recovery opportunity, not guaranteed collection.
- Include security, data protection, and retention controls in the proposal pack.
